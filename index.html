Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to start a Consultation with a doctor?

    Press Book a Appointment and Create an account. Then Add money to wallet and choose a doctor in speciality wise and book a slot. Login 5 mins prior to your appointment slot and start your consultation

  • How many hours prior can I book slot and is there emergency services?

    One hour prior you can book the slot. No emergency services. In future we will be adding emergency appointments too

  • What if I cancel my appointment or missed my time slot? will amount be refunded back?

    IF you cancel your appointment then 50 percent amount will be refunded to wallet. Whereas if you missed your time slot then Doctor would be available in till end of your allotted time slot after which consultation would end and 100 percent amount will not be refunded. Money cannot be transferred back to bank account. You can use it for another consultation

  • What if I was asked to review another day after consultation?

    One review consultation if booked within 7 days from first consultation is free,this consulatation cannot be rescheduled or cancel by Doctor/patient.

  • How to share the blood reports that was requested by doctor with him?

    Kindly take a snapshot/scanned copy of the report and mail it to contact@surgerycare.com with patient details and doctor details

  • What is Health workshop and when is it conducted? What is the cost?

    Health workshops are one to one session where medical practitioner will get to know about your lifestyle and get to analysis risk factor in your daily life and can come up with lifestyle modifications to prevent the future complications. Health workshops are conducted at regular intervals with limited seats. For enquiry and book your slot by DM in whatsapp 86103 23843. As each package is personalised kindly DM 86103 23843 or reach us at contact@surgerycare.com to know the cost

  • Can I consult for my family members with same account?

    Yes. You need to add your family members details in your account and consult with same account and same wallet

  • What if I am living outside India and need consultation?

    Yes. You can book consultation even if you are NRI, but Prescription will not be provided. Only health advisory can be given. For drugs you need to consult nearby physician only

  • What if doctor is late for consultation or if didn’t turn up for appointment or if doctor himself cancels appointment?

    If doctor is held up in previous consultation, you will get SMS notification regarding delay in schedule. If you didn’t get any notification from us and doctor didn’t turn up in the appointment time or doctor cancels appointment time then 100 percent amount will be refunded to wallet

  • What if I got any allergic reaction to any prescribed drug?

    Surgerycare or the prescribing doctor will not be responsible for any reaction to the drug that is prescribed unless the particular drug that you are allergic to is mentioned in medical history

  • How to verify the genuineness of doctors listed here?

    We Surgerycare team will verify the doctor’s qualification and its authenticity and then only doctor name will be listed. You can verify from NMC Medical council website too.

  • Can I get medical Certificate/ physical fitness certificate for the condition which I got treated by Surgerycare?

    No. Medical certificate or physical fitness certificate will not be provided

  • What if I forgot password or Username?

    You can reset password using reset password option. But if you forgot Username then you have register with new username. Wallet amount of that username will not be refunded.

  • What if the prescribed drug is not available in my nearby medical shop?

    You can search place order in various online pharmacy website. You can also request us to change medication by reaching us at contact@surgerycare.com

  • How to use discount coupon code?

    If you have Discount Coupon code, then refill the wallet with consultation amount and finish the consultation. Then sent the payment receipt with your coupon code to support@Surgerycare.com and get the discount amount back to your wallet within 5-7 working days. You can use that amount in your next consultation.
